About & Contact

Why This Exists

I built this as a swim parent who wanted a quicker way to find pool, team, and meet information during the busy summer season.

What It Brings Together

I bring commonly needed Columbia Association outdoor pool and Columbia Neighborhood Swim League information together in one place for families, swimmers, and volunteers.

A Commitment to Free Access

This app is free, and I intend to keep it that way. My goal is simple: make pool visits and summer swim plans a little easier for our community.

About App Information

I do my best to keep the app current with official Columbia Association and CNSL publications. If something does not match an official source, please include a link to that source in your message. I can review what the app displays, but I cannot change information published by CA or CNSL. Because I maintain this community project in my spare time, replies and updates may take a little while.

Thank you! 💙 This app is a labor of love. I enjoy working on it, and I hope it helps.
