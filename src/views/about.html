About This Project

A quicker path to summer swim information

This project brings Columbia Association outdoor pool information and Columbia Neighborhood Swim League (CNSL) team and meet details together in one simple place. Its goal is to make it easier for pool users, parents, swimmers, and teams to find the information they need quickly during a busy summer season.

Built from a parent's perspective

The app was created by a swim parent who wanted a faster, more practical way to check pool schedules, locate team information, and plan around meets. It is designed for common questions such as where to swim today, where a team practices, or when the next meet takes place.

Community convenience, not an official source

This is an independent community project. It is not operated by or affiliated with Columbia Association (CA) or CNSL. Information is gathered from publicly available CA and CNSL materials; please verify important schedule and event details with their official sources.

Respectful of your privacy

The app does not collect personally identifiable information. Preferences such as your appearance setting, favorite pool, and favorite team are stored only in your browser on your device. General, anonymized site usage may be measured to understand whether the app is useful and where it can improve.

What I hope this becomes

I hope this remains a dependable, free community tool that removes a little friction from pool visits and summer swim participation: less searching across pages and documents, and more time enjoying the pool and supporting our teams.
