About

Made for Columbia Summer Swim

Why this exists

This project was created by a swim parent who wanted a quicker way to find pool, team, and meet information during the summer season.

What it brings together

It puts commonly needed Columbia Association outdoor pool and Columbia Neighborhood Swim League information in one convenient place for families, swimmers, and volunteers.

A commitment to free access

This app is free and will remain free. It is designed to be practical and easy to use while families plan pool visits and summer swim activities.
