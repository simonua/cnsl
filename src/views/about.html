About

Made for Columbia Summer Swim

Why this exists

I built this as a swim parent who wanted a quicker way to find pool, team, and meet information during the busy summer season.

What it brings together

I bring commonly needed Columbia Association outdoor pool and Columbia Neighborhood Swim League information together in one place for families, swimmers, and volunteers.

A commitment to free access

This app is free, and I intend to keep it that way. My goal is simple: make pool visits and summer swim plans a little easier for our community.
