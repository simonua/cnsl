Welcome to the CA Pool & CNSL Assistant!

Spend less time searching and more time at the pool!

  • Find pools by features and opening hours. A one-of-a-kind way to choose the right pool.
  • Keep swim team days organized. Find practices, meets, and meet-day guidance in one place.
  • Stay current. Pool schedules are updated shortly after CA publishes updates.
  • Save a favorite pool and team, and set other preferences.
  • Accessible by design. Use clear, keyboard-friendly controls with screen-reader support.
  • Use it your way. Visit the website or install the app. It works offline except for live weather.

Free and private: Always free and anonymous. Your preferences stay on your device, and no personal data is tracked.
