Version 2.0.0 - May 25, 2026

This release prepared the app for the {{ YEAR }} season and introduced new ways to find, personalize, and trust current information:

  • Ready for the {{ YEAR }} season: Pool hours, CNSL team information, and meet schedules have been updated for the {{ YEAR }} outdoor pool season, with links back to published sources when you need to double-check details.
  • Share pool information easily: The home page now includes quick links for sharing the site by text message, email, or Facebook so families can pass along pool and CNSL schedule information.
  • One dependable home address: Links, previews, installation details, and site-use measurement now consistently use {{ HOME_PAGE_HOSTNAME }} as the official home page address.
  • Improved accessibility: Navigation now offers a skip-to-content path and predictable keyboard focus when the menu is open. Pool, team, and meet directories announce when updated information is ready, and reduced-motion preferences are respected.
  • Settings without leaving your place: Settings now open over the page you are viewing and can be closed when you are done. Changes apply automatically without a save step, and the page underneath updates after the settings window closes.
  • Find a pool by its features: The Pools page now includes published facility features grouped for accessibility, young swimmers and non-swimmers, sports, water play, and amenities. Coordinated category colors provide an additional visual cue in light and dark themes while the visible group labels remain primary. Select what matters for your visit to narrow the list quickly.
  • A clearer weekly view of pool schedules: Pool schedule details can now be shown in a weekly calendar view, making public swim hours, practices, special schedules, and closures easier to compare across the week. When the calendar is open, it automatically brings today's column into view. Choose your preferred view on the Settings page.
  • Your favorites come first: You can select a favorite pool and favorite swim team in Settings so the information you use most is easier to reach. Pool and team directories remember whether you expanded or collapsed your favorite tile. The home page shows the next seven days of published practices and meets for a selected favorite team, and on meet days its matchup appears first in the meet schedule. Choices are saved on your device.
  • Location stays in your control: Distance estimates on the Pools page are now off by default. Enable location awareness in Settings only when you want your browser to share your current location for distance estimates, then optionally sort nearby pools by distance.
  • More team details: The Teams page includes available team staff, verified regular-practice times and locations, and official team-calendar links where published, helping families find the people and schedule details connected with their team.
  • Faster team logo loading: Team logos are now delivered in one display-sized, high-resolution image, reducing downloads while keeping logos clear on high-density screens.
  • Install it for convenient access: A compact Phone Install option near the bottom of the home page expands only when you need it, with direct installation on supported Android browsers and clearer steps for iPhone and iPad. After content has loaded, key pool, team, and meet information remains available when your connection is limited or offline.
  • More dependable offline access: After content has loaded, the installed app preserves a working experience when optional resources are unavailable and shows a clear offline page when a page has not been saved for offline access yet.
  • Safer links from published information: Pool, team, and meet pages now validate public website, map, email, and telephone links before presenting them, while safely displaying published directory text.
  • Purpose-limited analytics with clearer privacy disclosure: The deployed site uses Google Analytics only for purpose-limited site and feature usage reporting, such as page visits and interaction categories. Analytics storage is enabled so Google Analytics can count visits and sessions using its own first-party analytics identifiers, while advertising personalization and Google signals are disabled. App-defined measurement does not send saved settings values, selected pools or teams, contact details, current coordinates, free-form content, or external destination details. Browser security rules also limit which external services the app may contact.
  • Weather safety alerts when storms matter: From one hour before today's first published pool activity until the last pool closes, a site-wide banner appears when National Weather Service alerts or the near-term forecast indicate thunderstorms or other hazardous conditions. The warning label is always visible, desktop visitors see its full message, and mobile visitors can minimize the detail while keeping quick access to Columbia Association's live pool status directory. Its refresh frequency can be selected in Settings.
  • Watching official sources for changes: Published pool, team, and meet sources are monitored during the active season so updates can be reviewed and reflected in the app. The FAQ identifies the official public sources used as the source of truth and reports when accepted seasonal data was last checked; confirm time-sensitive details there before you head out.
  • Easier to find current information: Search and shared-link previews now distinguish pool, team, meet, and volunteer resource pages more clearly, helping families reach the relevant {{ YEAR }} information faster.
