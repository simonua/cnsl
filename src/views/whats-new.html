  • Pool Meet Team Ready for the {{ YEAR }} season: Pool hours, team information, and meet schedules are updated for the {{ YEAR }} outdoor season. Links to official sources help you double-check details.
  • Check when official sources were reviewed: The web app watches official pool, team, and meet sources during the season. The FAQ lists those sources and the last review date. Please check time-sensitive details before you leave.
  • Pool See weather safety alerts during pool hours: A banner appears when National Weather Service alerts or the forecast warn of unsafe weather. The warning stays visible and links to Columbia Association's live pool status. Choose how often it updates in Settings.
  • Pool Meet Team Put your favorites first: Choose a favorite pool and team in Settings. Home then shows the team's next seven days of practices and meets, and its matchup appears first on meet day. Your choices stay on this device.
  • Pool Find pools by their features: Filter the Pools page for accessibility, young-swimmer, sports, water-play, and other features. Clear names and colors help separate the groups.
  • Pool Compare a full week of pool schedules: The weekly calendar puts public swimming, practices, special hours, and closures side by side. It opens to today, and you can choose this view in Settings.
  • Team Find more team details: The Teams page includes available staff, confirmed practice times and locations, and official calendar links.
  • Pool Keep control of your location: Distances on the Pools page are off by default. Turn them on in Settings only when you want your device to share your location for distance results. The web app does not save or send your location.
  • Change Settings without losing your place: Settings opens over the current page. Changes apply when you close it, with no Save button needed.
  • Use saved pages without internet: After a page loads, the installed web app can keep working when some information is unavailable. If a page was not saved, you see a clear offline message.
  • Use the web app more easily with assistive tools: You can skip to the main content, keyboard focus stays in the open menu, page updates are announced, and reduced-motion choices are respected.
  • Install the web app: Phone Install gives direct Android installation when available and clear steps for iPhone and iPad. Key pool, team, and meet information remains available with a weak or missing connection after it loads.
  • Open published links more safely: Pool, team, and meet pages check website, map, email, and phone links before showing them.
  • Keep usage reporting limited: Google Analytics counts visits and general feature use. Advertising and personalized tracking are off. The web app does not send saved settings, favorite pools or teams, contact details, your location, text you enter, or outside links you open.
  • Share pool information easily: Home now offers text message, email, and Facebook links for sharing pool and CNSL schedules.
  • Find current information more easily: Search results and shared links now make pool, team, meet, and volunteer pages easier to tell apart.
  • Use one official home address: Links, previews, installation details, and visit counts now use {{ HOME_PAGE_HOSTNAME }}.
  • Team Load team logos faster: Team logos now use one clear image sized for the screen, which reduces downloading.
