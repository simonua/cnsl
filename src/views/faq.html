CNSL & Columbia Association Pool FAQs | Columbia, MD

Frequently Asked Questions

What is CNSL?

CNSL stands for Columbia Neighborhood Swim League, a summer recreational swim league in Columbia, MD.

How do I find my team's practice schedule?

Visit the Teams page to find your team's practice schedule and other information.

Where are meets held?

Meets are held at team home pools. Visit the Meets page for details on upcoming meets and locations.

Where does this app get its data, and when was it checked?

Pool information is transcribed from publicly available Columbia Association (CA) publications. Team and meet information is transcribed from publicly available official CNSL publications. These official public sources are the source of truth for this web app.

Information shared through social media, email, messaging groups, or other community channels may be helpful, but it is not treated as authoritative here unless it is reflected in an official public source.

How can my team request a data update?

Team representatives can email simonkurtz@gmail.com with the subject "CNSL Data Update" and the corrected information or published source link.

How can I request a feature?

Have an idea that would make this app more useful? Email simonkurtz@gmail.com with the subject "CNSL Feature Request" and a brief description of what would help.

Is this an official web app?

No. It is not operated by or affiliated with Columbia Association (CA). CA does not provide an API for publicly-available data and directs users to their websites and app. This web app was created by a swim parent, who wanted an easier, quicker, and more tailored path to get pool and team information.

Can I add this to my phone's home screen like an app?

Yes! This website is a Progressive Web App (PWA) that can be added to your home screen for easy access. It is fully functioning without an internet connection as all team, pool, and meet data is part of the web app.

On iPhone/iPad:
1. Open this website in Safari
2. Tap the Share button (square with arrow pointing up)
3. Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
4. Give it a name (like "CNSL") and tap "Add"

On Android:
1. Open this website in Chrome
2. Tap the three-dot menu (⋮)
3. Tap "Add to Home screen" or "Install app"
4. Give it a name and tap "Add"

Once added, you can launch it from your home screen just like any other app! It will work offline for previously viewed content.

What data do you track or keep?

No personally identifiable information (PII) is tracked.
This web app uses anonymous aggregate Google Analytics measurement to help me generally understand usage (page hits, sharing actions, and changed public favorite choices). Analytics storage, advertising personalization, Google signals, referrers, and page query details are disabled or excluded by the app. That measurement is intended to describe site use rather than identify a visitor. The web app is free. Maintaining and hosting it on GitHub Pages is free. I do not intend to monetize with ads, subscriptions, purchases, selling data (which I don't track anyway), or any other means. This web app exists for the community and is a labor of love that exists solely for your and my convenience as we all navigate yet another busy summer.

Settings are saved in your browser on your device. When you change a favorite team or favorite pool, that fixed public directory choice is counted in anonymous aggregate usage measurement; visitor names, staff or contact information, appearance, location awareness, current coordinates, and free-form content are not sent through analytics. Public feature-filter choices would be disclosed here before they are measured. You can remove local choices at any time from the Settings page or by clearing this site's browser data.

You can also read Google Analytics guidance on PII.
