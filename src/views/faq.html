CNSL & Columbia Association Pool FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Using the App

What is CNSL?

CNSL stands for Columbia Neighborhood Swim League, a summer recreational swim league in Columbia, MD.

How do I find a pool schedule, team practice, or meet?

Use the Pools page for outdoor pool information, the Teams page for team and practice details, and the Meets page for meet dates and host pools.

What time zone does the app use?

All dates and times in the app are shown in Eastern Daylight Time (EDT).

Why do meet locations show lane and course measurements?

Lane counts and course measurements can help families and teams understand a meet's setup. They also provide useful context when comparing times from meter and yard pools. For official course terminology and rules, see USA Swimming Rules & Policies. Columbia Association and CNSL remain the official sources for local pool and meet information.

Can I install the app and use it offline?

Yes. You can add the app to your phone or tablet's home screen. After you have opened the app online, key pool, team, and meet information remains available when your connection is limited or unavailable.

On iPhone or iPad:

  1. Open this website in Safari.
  2. Tap the Share button, then select "Add to Home Screen."
  3. Choose a name and tap "Add."

On Android:

  1. Open this website in Chrome.
  2. Open the menu and select "Add to Home screen" or "Install app."
  3. Choose a name and confirm the installation.

Privacy and Preferences

What data is measured or stored?

You do not need an account or need to share your name or contact details. Your preferences stay in your browser. Google Analytics uses its own first-party identifier to provide combined reports about visits and general feature use. The app reports whether each session uses a webpage or an installed app. A fixed campaign label is also included when someone opens the app from its published flyer QR code or a Text, Email, or Facebook link shared from the app. Advertising personalization and Google signals are disabled.

The app includes selected favorite pools and teams in aggregate reports after checking each choice against the published directory. Pool page and schedule link clicks include the public pool identifier and a fixed action label after checking the pool against the current directory. Other external-link clicks include a fixed destination label, such as Columbia Association, Google Maps, email, or phone call, but not the full address, query, message, email address, or phone number. It does not send Google Analytics your name, contact details, account information, current location, other saved choices, filters, free-form text, or referring page. A fixed label from an app-published campaign link may show how visitors found the app, and that label is removed from the address in your browser. You can also read Google's guidance about personally identifiable information in Analytics.

Does the app use my location?

Distance estimates are off by default. If you turn them on in Settings, your browser shares your current location only long enough to calculate nearby pool distances. The app does not track, save, or send your location.

Does weather safety use my current location?

No. Weather checks use one fixed point for the Columbia pool area, not your current location. You can see the same National Weather Service information here:

  • Active weather alerts (data only)
  • Local forecast information (data only)
  • Local weather information (web)

Optional location access is separate and is used only to estimate distances to pools. Even when you allow it, the app does not track, save, or send your location.

Where are my preferences saved?

Your settings stay in the browser on your device. Combined analytics reports may show that a type of setting was changed. Favorite pool and team changes also include the selected public pool name or team identifier, or that the favorite was cleared. Pool feature filter changes include the selected public feature names, or that the filters were cleared; other setting choices are not included. You can remove your preferences from the Settings page or by clearing this site's browser data.
