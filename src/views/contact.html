About App Information

The app's data may not always be perfect, but I do my best to keep it up to date with official Columbia Association and CNSL documentation. If something does not match an official source, please include a link to that source in your message. I can review what the app displays, but I cannot change information published by CA or CNSL. Because this community project is maintained in my spare time, replies and updates may not be immediate.

Thank you! 💙 This app is a labor of love. I enjoy working on it, and I hope it helps!
