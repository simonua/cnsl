## Source Authority And Currency

Pool information is accepted only from official public Columbia Association pages and documents. Meet and team information is accepted from official public CNSL publication and team pages. Recurring home-meet guidance for all attendees, the home team, or the visiting team may also be accepted from a directly supplied team-manager communication when its sender, team, and role in the represented meet are known; this guidance may include role-specific arrival and warmup times. Only operational facts intended for participating families are transcribed, and personal sender information is not published. Meet-specific operational guidance is not modeled. Social media posts, forwarded messages, messaging groups, and other community-shared content are not source evidence for application data unless the information is published through an official public source.
