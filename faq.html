Common Questions

What is CNSL?

The Columbia Neighborhood Swim League (CNSL) is a summer swim program for children ages 5-18 who live in or near Columbia, Maryland.

How do I join a swim team?

Each Columbia Association neighborhood has its own swim team. Visit the Teams page to find your local team and their practice schedule.

When is the swim season?

The CNSL swim season generally runs from late May through late July, with meets typically on Saturday mornings.

Where can I find the meet schedule?

The current season's meet schedule is available on the Meets page. You can also check your team's specific page for their meet schedule.
